# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Rhythm-game players using DTXManiaRS at a desk or seated behind an electronic drum kit. They need to browse songs, confirm a chart, prepare their controls and presentation, play, practice, and review results without unnecessary navigation or hard-to-read controls.

## Product Purpose

DTXManiaRS is a modern DTX rhythm-game client that preserves proven DTXMania mechanics while redesigning the surrounding experience for responsive, understandable play. Success means players can move from song discovery to a correctly configured performance with confidence, whether they use a keyboard or a drum kit.

## Brand Personality

Precise, energetic, confident.

## Anti-references

Avoid interfaces that expose every capability at once, rely on unexplained controls, or make routine actions difficult to find. Arcade references may inform structure and distance readability, but DTXManiaRS should not copy their visual clutter or franchise-specific decoration. Keep the current product simple while leaving clear extension points for future depth.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next action obvious from a distance.
2. Use progressive disclosure: quick choices first, detailed editing on demand.
3. Show live system state, especially selected chart, active setup, connected input, and readiness.
4. Preserve expert speed with direct navigation and remembered choices.
5. Add future capability through focused surfaces instead of crowding existing ones.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No additional accessibility requirements are committed for the current phase. The architecture should avoid preventing later accessibility work.
