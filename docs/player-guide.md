# Player Guide

Purpose: maintained instructions for installing, configuring, and playing the
drums game.

Audience: keyboard and electronic-drum players.

Status: Maintained. Detailed verified workflows are being completed in Cycle 8.

Neighboring guides: [roadmap](roadmap.md), [compatibility](compatibility.md),
and [data and persistence](data-and-persistence.md).

## Guide ownership

This guide owns player controls and workflows: song discovery, input profiles,
MIDI setup, calibration, normal play, pause, Practice transport, Results
analysis, and recommended practice loops. Format/media support belongs in the
[compatibility guide](compatibility.md); storage, backup, and recovery belong in
the [data and persistence guide](data-and-persistence.md).

Verified commands and complete workflows will be populated before Cycle 8 is
marked complete. No hardware behavior is claimed without a corresponding
manual-check label.
